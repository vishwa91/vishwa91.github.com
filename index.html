Hello there! I am a PhD student in the ECE department at Carnegie Mellon University. I work with Prof. Aswin Sankaranarayanan.

I work on ...

... various image acquisition, compressive sensing and computational photography problems with special emphasis on co-design of imaging hardware and algorithms.
Specifically, I work on camera design for hyperspectral imaging and related inference techniques. My broaad goal is to bring several of the inference algorithms on hyperspectral images to optical domain to increase speed as well as reduce storage requrements.
