Hello there! I am a postdoctoral researcher in the EE department at Rice University. I work with Prof. Richard Baraniuk and Prof. Ashok Veeraraghavan where I was a Rice Future Faculty Fellow from 2020 to 2021.

Research Interests

My research interests are in the area of computational imaging where I co-design optical systems and algorithms to sense, represent, and infer from high dimensional visual signals such as videos, hyperspectral images, and lightfields. My research encompasses a broad set of disciplines including optics, meta-materials, algorithms, machine learning, and signal processing.

You can find more about my research here

Previous education

I did my doctoral research with Prof. Aswin Sankaranarayanan from Carnegie Mellon University. My thesis was on Spectrally-programmable cameras for imaging and inference. I was a recipient of the outstanding teaching assistant in the ECE department in 2018, the Prabhu and Poonam Goel graduate fellowship award in 2019, and the A. G. Jordan award for outstanding thesis in the ECE departmant in 2020. My thesis was also a finalist for the ACM SIGGRAPH 2020 thesis fast forward.

Prior to CMU, I was an undergraduate in the EE department at Indian Institute of Technology Madras. I worked with Prof. A N Rajagopalan on several image enhancement algorithms on mobile device. I graduated in 2014 with Honors and a minor in Operations Research.
