Accessible Reading Material

The project is under the guidance of Dr. Anil Prabhakar and Dr. Sujatha Srinivasan. The aim is to make a low cost plotter that will make simple children's picture books tactilable. The project is called Tactograph, after Tactiling Pantograph. The plotter is a 5 links robot, with two stepper motors as actuators. The device is based on PIC18F4550 USB device. Patent pending (Patent Application No. 4091/CHE/ 2011“Tactograph”Date of Entry 28th November 2011 in the names ofIndian Institute of Technology- Madras and Chetana Charitable Trust).

The project has won 3rd prize in IEEE AIYEHUM competition and selected for IEEE EPICS funding program. The project was a part of Innovative Students Project(IC&SR, IITMadras).

Porting Boot 2 Gecko

Working on Bachmann platform

(Summer 2013 at Maschinenfabrik Reinhausen, Regensburg, Germany)

The internship involved porting current software to the nwe Bachmann PLC system and creating automation tools for the framework. Broady, I worked on the following:

  • XML validation script in python for module configuration
  • Porting Voltage Interrupt Monitoring hardware to PLC system
  • Simulation module for transformer parameters
  • Module catalog generation automation

Apart from the academic part of the project, the summer internship was an amazing cultural experience. It was the first time I had been abroad and I was impressed by the discipline of the country. I was working with some amazing people in the company, in a beautiful country. I have documented all my experiences at my blog. The home page has some pictures from my euro trip at the top.
